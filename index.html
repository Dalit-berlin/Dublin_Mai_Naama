Event Structure

The event lasts about an hour and a half. Entry and exit are possible throughout the event.

The event consists of three parts, which are different from one another but also similar. You may watch any one of the segments, part of a segment, or all three segments together.

During the event, we will have two ten-minute breaks.

Arrive, stay, leave, and return according to the rhythm that suits your visit.
